Prove (or disprove) the strict inclusions NC-A₂ ⊊ NC-D₄ ⊊ NC-E₈. In particular: is there a decision problem solvable in polynomial-depth E₈ null circuits but NOT in polynomial-depth D₄ circuits? This would be a null-geometric analogue of P ≠ BQP (if true).

Propose a physical system (photonic, condensed matter, or other) in which the NULBIT state space S² × U(1) is realised and the gates {NC, NI, NP} can be physically implemented.
